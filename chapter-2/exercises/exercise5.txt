Which of the following are not legal C identifiers?
(a) 100_bottles
(b) _100_bottles
(c) one__hundred__bottles
(d) bottles_by_the_hundred_

a isn't legal
b is legal but starting an identifier with an "_" isn't recommended.
c is legal but having two underscores back-to-back isn't recommended.
d is legal but you shouldn't end an identifier with an underscore.
